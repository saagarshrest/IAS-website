OUR STORY

Built for Students Who Never Give Up

Institute of Advanced Studies (IAS) was founded with a simple mission: to provide a pathway for students who face educational gaps, career professionals seeking advancement, and international students needing flexible options.

We understand that life doesn't always follow a linear path. Whether you've taken a break from studies, are working full-time, or studying abroad, we're here to help you complete your degree on your terms.

Our Mission

To make quality higher education accessible, flexible, and affordable for every student in Nepal, regardless of their circumstances, location, or life situation. We break down barriers to education.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's leading educational institution for non-traditional learners and working professionals, recognized for innovation, flexibility, and student success across South Asia.
