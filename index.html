ABOUT US

Empowering Education for Everyone

Institute of Advanced Studies (IAS) is Nepal's premier destination for flexible, affordable, and recognized Bachelor's and Master's degree programs. We're dedicated to helping gap students, dropouts, professionals, and international students continue their educational journey without compromises.

Our Mission
